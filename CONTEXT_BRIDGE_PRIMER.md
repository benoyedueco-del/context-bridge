# Context Bridge™ — Receiving AI Primer
**Version:** 2.0
**Use this when:** You are starting a new conversation with any AI and giving it one or more Context Bridge documents to onboard from.

---

## Instructions

Paste this at the start of your new conversation, before or alongside your Context Bridge document(s).

---

```
I am giving you one or more CONTEXT BRIDGE™ documents.
These contain the complete intelligence transfer from my previous AI sessions on this project.

============================================================
YOUR INSTRUCTIONS BEFORE RESPONDING
============================================================

1. READ EVERY DOCUMENT COMPLETELY before you respond to anything.
   Do not skim. Do not respond until you have processed everything.

2. DO NOT ask me to re-explain anything covered in the bridge documents.
   If it is in the bridge, you already know it. Act accordingly.

3. RESPECT ALL NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES listed in the bridges.
   These are hard constraints, not suggestions. Treat violations as errors.

4. DO NOT suggest approaches listed in REJECTED PATHS.
   These have already been considered and dismissed. Do not re-propose them.

5. START FROM CURRENT STATE.
   Do not start from the beginning. Do not recap history unless I ask.

6. HONOR HUMAN REVIEW FLAGS.
   Items marked [UNCERTAIN] or [INFERRED] need my confirmation before you act on them.
   Ask me about these specifically — do not assume.

7. IF ANYTHING IS UNCLEAR OR CONTRADICTORY, ask me.
   But be specific: tell me exactly what conflicts or what is ambiguous.
   Do not ask vague clarifying questions.

============================================================
AFTER READING — CONFIRM BEFORE WE BEGIN
============================================================

Once you have read all bridge documents, respond with ONLY these four things:

1. PROJECT: What you understand this project to be (one paragraph, your words).

2. CURRENT STATE: What you understand the current state to be (bullet points).

3. IMMEDIATE NEXT ACTION: What you understand the immediate next action to be (one sentence).

4. CLARIFICATIONS NEEDED: Any conflicts or uncertainties you want me to resolve before we begin.
   If none, say "None — ready to proceed."

Do not begin project work until I confirm your understanding is correct.
```

---

## Tips

- If giving multiple bridges, paste them in chronological order (oldest first)
- Tell the AI which bridge is most recent: *"The bridge dated 2026-03-18 is the most current state"*
- If the AI skips the confirmation step and jumps straight to work, redirect it: *"Stop — confirm your understanding first as instructed"*
